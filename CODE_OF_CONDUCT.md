# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The full text is maintained at the link above and is incorporated here by
reference rather than reproduced in full.

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for
everyone. Participants are expected to behave professionally, treat others
with respect, and act in good faith.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a
[GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/coryhacking/wavefoundry/discussions)
or contacting the project maintainer directly via GitHub. All reports will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
